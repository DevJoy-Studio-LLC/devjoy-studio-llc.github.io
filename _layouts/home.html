We are building a journaling app

It's well known that journaling bring many benefits. But when paired with a work log, a journal becomes an incredibly useful tool for the whole team.

Decisions are knowledge

Easily search the journal to remember why you or a teammate made an important tactical design decision many moons ago.

Better annual reviews

Never lose track of what you worked on. During you annual performance review, recall with ease that big project you finished eleven months ago!

Stay connected

Nothing is more motivating than knowing everyone is pushing in the same direction. Through the team work log, see how you and your coworkers align in pursuing your team's objectives.
